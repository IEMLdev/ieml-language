Comments Relations Table M:O:. Words

M x O generates nouns representing six kinds of actors or agents. Note the system of analogies between actions and agents: each actor is semantically connected to the action that results from an inverse multiplication: idea/know, message/say, subject/want, individual/commit, object/can, body/do. Beyond the six actions and six actors, the letters coming respectively from O\otimesM and M\otimesO can also be used to represent dialectics or symmetries of six concepts: hexads.
